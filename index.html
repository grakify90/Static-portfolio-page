Introduction

Gachapin (ガチャピン) is a popular Japanese television character who appeared in the children's program Hirake! Ponkikki and its succeeding series, Ponkickies on Fuji TV.
He is a green, bucktoothed dinosaur and is usually accompanied by his friend Mukku, a red Yeti.

Fun Facts

  • Gachapin is a child dinosaur.
  • Gachapin is 5 years old and his birthday is on April 2nd.
  • Gachapin was born on a tropical island.
  • He is espcially good at sports.
  • The energy balls on his wrists enable him to fly and dive underwater.
